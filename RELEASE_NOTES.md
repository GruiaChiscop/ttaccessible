The Connected Users window now shows everyone — including people who haven't joined a channel — and your usual keyboard shortcuts work right inside it.

## Connected Users window

- **People not in a channel now show up too** — the **Connected Users** window (Command-Shift-W) used to list only people who had joined a channel. It now lists everyone on the server, including those who just connected and haven't entered a channel yet — which was the whole point of the window.
- **Keyboard shortcuts and actions now work in the window** — when the Connected Users window is focused, your usual per-person shortcuts act on the person selected there: get information (Command-I), mute or unmute (Command-Shift-M), adjust volume (Command-U), make or revoke channel operator (Control-Command-O), move someone (Command-Option-X), remove from the channel or the server (Command-K, Command-Shift-K), and manage what you receive from them. No need to switch back to the main window.

## Install

If you're already running ttaccessible, it will install this update for you — no action needed.

Manual install:

1. Download `ttaccessible-1.5.1-30.zip` below.
2. Unzip and drag `ttaccessible.app` into your `/Applications` folder, replacing the previous version.
3. Double-click — no Gatekeeper warning thanks to notarization.
